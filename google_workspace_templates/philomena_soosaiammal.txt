Philomena Soosaiammal

Philomena Soosaiammal (18 September 1915 – 10 April 1998) was a prominent homemaker and philanthropist in Thanjavur.

Family Life
She was married to the political leader and philanthropist A. Y. S. Parisutha Nadar. She was the cornerstone of the household, supporting his extensive public career and raising their children, including their sons S. P. Selvaraj and S. P. Anthonisamy.

Charitable Initiatives
Philomena was known for her active participation in local social organizations, including the Guild of Service. She quietly funded numerous scholarships for underprivileged students and organized local food distribution programs in Thanjavur, leaving a legacy of kindness and generosity.
