Gnanammal Nadar

Gnanammal Nadar (10 September 1885 – 12 April 1960) was a prominent matriarch and community patron in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu.

Family & Life
Gnanammal was married to the merchant and philanthropist A. Yagappa Nadar. Together, they raised three children: a daughter, Idhayameri Ammal, and two sons, A. Y. Arulanthasamy Nadar and A. Y. S. Parisutha Nadar.

Social Work
Gnanammal was actively involved in community and church welfare services in Thanjavur. She supported local orphanages and helped run parish charity guilds, establishing a family tradition of social responsibility and civic philanthropy.
