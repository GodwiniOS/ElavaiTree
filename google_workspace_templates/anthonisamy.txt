S. P. Anthonisamy

S. P. Anthonisamy (Born: 20 November 1945) is a prominent educationist, technologist, and institutional leader based in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu. He is the son of A. Y. S. Parisutha Nadar and Philomena Soosaiammal.

Educational Institutions & Leadership
- Parisutham Institute of Technology and Science: S. P. Anthonisamy is the Founder and Chairman of the Parisutham Institute of Technology and Science (PITS) in Thanjavur. The engineering institution was established to provide high-quality technical education, research opportunities, and career-readiness training in the delta region.
- Development Philosophy: He advocates for holistic student development, combining rigorous engineering education with sports, communication skills, and community outreach.

Philanthropy
In coordination with his family and the A. Y. S. Parisutha Nadar Centenary Foundation, he funds scholarships for low-income engineering students and sponsors local rural technological literacy camps.
