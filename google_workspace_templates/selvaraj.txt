S. P. Selvaraj

S. P. Selvaraj (Born: 20 March 1942) is a prominent entrepreneur, hotelier, educationist, and philanthropist based in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu. He is the son of political leader A. Y. S. Parisutha Nadar and Philomena Soosaiammal.

Business Career
S. P. Selvaraj is the Managing Director of Hotel Gnanam, a leading hospitality establishment in Thanjavur, known for serving travelers and tourists visiting the heritage temple town.

Educational & Social Leadership
- Gnanam School of Business: He serves as the Chairman of the Gnanam School of Business (GSB), which has established itself as a premier management institution in Tamil Nadu.
- Civic Engagement: S. P. Selvaraj has held leadership roles in several social and humanitarian organizations. He is an active member and past president of the Rotary Club of Thanjavur and has been heavily involved in the local YMCA chapter, sponsoring numerous community development initiatives.
- Centenary Foundation: Through the A. Y. S. Parisutha Nadar Centenary Foundation, he directs annual scholarships, social welfare schemes, and the prestigious memorial lecture series honoring his father's legacy.
