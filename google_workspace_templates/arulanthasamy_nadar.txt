A. Y. Arulanthasamy Nadar

A. Y. Arulanthasamy Nadar (14 February 1905 – 30 August 1978) was the eldest son of A. Yagappa Nadar and Gnanammal, and a prominent merchant, sports patron, and philanthropist in Thanjavur.

Career & Trade
Arulanthasamy managed the family's expanding commercial interests and agricultural estates in the Tanjore delta. Under his leadership, the family business expanded into new trade routes within the presidency.

Civic Leadership & Philanthropy
He was dedicated to youth development and public welfare in Thanjavur:
- Sports Patronage: He funded local athletic clubs, built youth football fields, and organized regional tournaments, advocating for sports as a means of character building.
- Land & Infrastructure: He supported the family's land donations for the Thanjavur Medical College and funded construction of classrooms for community schools in Thanjavur.
