Idhayameri Ammal

Idhayameri Ammal (12 May 1907 – 4 November 1989) was the eldest child and only daughter of A. Yagappa Nadar and Gnanammal. She was a pioneering educationist and social worker in Thanjavur.

Career in Education
After completing her training at Madras Teachers Training College, she joined the faculty of St. Joseph Girls High School in Thanjavur. She served as a dedicated teacher and local education organizer, committing her life to improving literacy rates and encouraging higher education among young women.

Social Contribution
She established local parish welfare committees to support low-income families and organized community study centers to assist students in Thanjavur.
